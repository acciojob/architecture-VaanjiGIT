Beautiful Architecture Around the World

Modern Architecture

Modern architecture focuses on simplicity, functionality, and the use of new materials like glass and steel. It emphasizes clean lines, open spaces, and minimal ornamentation. This style is widely seen in urban skyscrapers and contemporary homes.

Classical Architecture

Classical architecture is inspired by ancient Greek and Roman structures. It features columns, symmetry, and decorative elements. Famous examples include temples, monuments, and government buildings that reflect timeless beauty and proportion.
